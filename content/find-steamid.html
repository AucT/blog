Find SteamID - just my html+php try to make my own steamid finder. You can input your steamid in few formats:

  • STEAM_0:1:55975235
  • 111XXXXXX (SteamID32/AccountID)
  • 765XXXXXXXXXXXXXX (SteamID64)
  • http://steamcommunity.com/profiles/765XXXXXXXXXXXXXX
  • http://steamcommunity.com/id//

Guide:

  • 1. Go to https://steamid.xyz/
  • 2. Input any format you know as listed above
  • 3. Press Enter or button "GO"
